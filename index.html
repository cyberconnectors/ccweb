Program

Cyber Pathways brings transparency into the variety jobs in cybersecurity.

Participants can explore up to 8 Cyber Paths of their choice in depth and connect with cybersecurity experts from industry, the public sector, and from research.

Student Cooperation

Cyber Pathways is deeply rooted at ETH and collaborates closely with student groups.

The event was originally organised by ETH Cyber Group Alumni in 2023. The ETH Cyber Group drives several initiatives and offer fantastic opportunities to students to experience core aspects also of practical cybersecurity already during their studies.

Organizers - About Cyber Connectors

Cyber Connectors is the organising body behind Cyber Pathways. It was founded after a successful first Cyber Pathways event in 2023, to provide continuity for the format, as well as to further support Cyber Professionals on their paths in the Cybersecurity domain. We are not-for-profit and working for the community on a voluntary basis.

ETH Cyber Group

Cyber Connectors addresses four main topics

Cultivating a Cyber Community

Building a vibrant network for security professionals to foster knowledge sharing, collaborations, and enhancing their impact in the cybersecurity world.

Navigating Cyber Careers

Guiding both new and experienced professionals through diverse cybersecurity career paths, highlighting journeys to roles like CIO, CISO, or expert levels of Threat Researcher, Engineer or Architect.

Bridging the Skills Gap

Connecting talent with opportunities to make cybersecurity careers more attractive and accessible, leading individuals to meaningful and fulfilling roles and supporting technical growth.

Valuing Professional Worth

Launching a 'Cyber Positioning System' for professionals to evaluate their market value and career opportunities, prioritizing personal growth, coaching, and mentoring.
